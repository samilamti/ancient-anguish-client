What you get

  • A real terminal

    Truecolor ANSI, GMCP, MCCP, drag-to-select copy, clickable URLs, and a 5,000-line scrollback. Bold, italic, underline, inverse — all rendered.

  • Live game-state HUD

    30+ configurable elements: HP/SP/XP, combat aim/attack/defend, hunger/thirst/drunk, alignment, followers, reboot countdown. The client rebuilds your prompt and syncs it for you.

  • Quick Commands and D-Pad

    Tap-to-send buttons, eight-direction movement, and a target picker that learns from recent words. Combat loops and travel at thumb reach.

  • Aliases and Immersions

    Short keywords that expand with $0/$1/$2 substitution. Display-only triggers that highlight, gag, or play sounds. Strict no-bots design.

  • Floating social windows

    Chat and Tells as dockable, resizable panels — pinned to one edge, hidden from your main log, or mirrored alongside it.

  • Area-based ambient music

    The client detects your location and crossfades between tracks. Bring your own music per area.

  • Themes & accessibility

    RPG Dark (gold on wood), Classic Dark (green on black), High Contrast, and a fully editable Custom palette. Scalable fonts (8–32 pt), wrap slider, native VoiceOver.

  • Privacy by default

    No analytics. No tracking. No ads. The only network traffic is your single connection to the game — you control the host.

Direct connection

Prefer your own setup? The MUD lives at anguish.org port 2222 over plain telnet — any standard MUD client will connect. 

telnet anguish.org 2222

The dedicated client handles ANSI, prompt, font, and the HUD for you. The next section is for players tuning a third-party client by hand.
