Chosen's MUD Client

for Ancient Anguish

A modern, independent client for Ancient Anguish — the text-based multiplayer fantasy world that has been running continuously since 1991.

Available for iPhone, iPad, Mac, Windows, Linux, and Android. Full ANSI truecolor, telnet negotiation (NAWS, TTYPE, MCCP, GMCP), a configurable game-state HUD, Quick Commands, area-based ambient music, floating Chat and Tells windows, and four themes including a high-contrast palette built for low-vision players.

No analytics. No tracking. No ads. The only network traffic is the single connection to the MUD server — and you control the host.

Help & support

If you have a question about the app, found a bug, or want to suggest a feature, the Support page has every way to reach the developer.

About this app

This is an independent third-party client for the Ancient Anguish MUD, developed in coordination with the AA team and promoted on the game's official Connect page at anguish.org/connect.php. The game itself is a separate service, operated by its own team since 1991 — this app is a comfortable doorway into it.

Privacy

The short version: nothing leaves your device except your direct connection to the MUD server. See the full Privacy Policy.
